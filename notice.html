Important Notice
COVID-19 is a threat to everyone.
Thanks to Government's Initiatives and the collaboration of citizens, the spread of the disease is minimized.
However, to protect everyone's safety during their visit, we ask you to perform the tasks below:

Compulsory Face Masks:
You must wear face masks, covering your whole mouth and nose, during your visit. We do not provide nor sell them at the park to avoid wastage.

Social Distancing:
Practice social distancing (at least 2m) during line up and seating.

Sanitization:
Clean your hands frequently with soap or sanitizer. We do provides booths for this purpose.

Health Check:
Before entering, ensure that you have no COVID symptoms. Upon arrival, we will check your temperature.

Services:
Some services will be modified to comply with Government's Regulation.

We are obligated to remove violators from the vicinity in order to operate.

Thank you for your understanding.
