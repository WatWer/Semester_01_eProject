FAQS
Do you have baby strollers?
Yes, after you enter the park, you can find rows of baby strollers on the right. They are free of charge, and is sanitized before each use.

Is the park Wheel Chair Accessible?
Yes, although for some Games and Rides, it is only accessible for viewing.

Do you have a Medic Team?
Yes, we have a trained medic team for your safety.

Do you provide birthday party / service?
Yes, please give us a call, we will call you back for information.

What form of payment do you take?
We accept Cash, and some cards: VISA, MasterCard, and PayPal.

Can I bring my pets?
Yes, on the condition that they are always on leash, and you have to clean up after them. Read more on our Park's Policies.
