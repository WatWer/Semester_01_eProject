Park's Policies
Smoking / Vaping:
Smoking and Vaping are prohibited in the park. If you need a kick, please go on one of our Games / Rides.

Food and Drinks:
Outside food and drinks are prohibited from entering the park, as they can cause unwanted health issues.

Security:
Weapons, Firearms, Poisons, and other threats is prohibited in the park, we will scan your belongings before you enter the park.

Surveillance:
We have set up Cameras around the park for your safety (except inside private areas).

Belongings and Children
We are not responsible for your belongings and/or children, please keep them with you the whole time.

We reserve the right to refuse services to, and expulsion of, violators.
