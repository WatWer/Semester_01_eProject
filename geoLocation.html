How to get here:


The Entrance is on Lac Long Quan St.

From Hoa Binh St, turn right at Lac Long Quan St, the Entrance is on the right.

From Ong Ich Khiem St, go around the intersection and turn left into Lac Long Quan St, the Entrance is on the right.


Parking:

Go along the entry way and you will see parking lots on the right.
